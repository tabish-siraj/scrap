How it works

"Join us in simplifying scrap disposal, promoting sustainability, and redefining
recycling for a brighter future."

Book an appointment

Easily schedule a scrap pick-up by visiting our website or using our user-friendly app. With just a few clicks, you can set a date and time that works for you and provide your location details. We've made the booking process simple and convenient for you

Scrap Pick-Up and Evaluation

Our team will promptly arrive at your location to collect your scrap. We ensure a fair evaluation and measurement process, guaranteeing that you receive the right compensation for your materials. Transparency and fairness are at the core of our service.

Payment and Compensation

Your compensation is straightforward and efficient. We offer various payment options, including cash, credit, or others, ensuring you're compensated in a way that suits you. Our payment system is designed with transparency in mind.

Sorting and Recycling

Once your scrap is collected, it's transported to our state-of-the-art warehouse, where our experts sort it into recyclable and sellable materials. We're dedicated to eco-friendly practices that benefit the environment.

Delivery to Recyclers

The sorted materials are then delivered to our trusted recycling partners. This ensures that your scrap doesn't end up in a landfill and contributes to a cleaner, more sustainable environment. By using our service, you're actively participating in the recycling process and making a positive impact.
